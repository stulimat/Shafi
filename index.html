Yes. Every evaluation comes with the doctor's full name, credentials, and board certification. Our doctors are practicing US physicians.

Absolutely. Our AI processes medical records in any language and from any country. Even photos from your phone.

If you paid $39 for Ask a Doctor and decide you need a consultation, the $39 is credited toward the price. For example, the General Consultation would cost you only $80 more instead of $119.
Is my information secure?
Your privacy is our top priority. All data is encrypted and we comply with US HIPAA standards. We never share your data with third parties.
How long does it take to get the evaluation?
Typically within 24 hours of receiving your records and payment.
